Tales of Xillia • Elize Lutus

Elize Lutus

Special Ability Teepo Switching
Pressing Taunt will cause Elize to switch between Teepo ON and Teepo OFF. While Teepo is ON, Elize's spirit artes are enhanced and her normal attacks become ranged. While Teepo is OFF, Elize's martial artes are enhanced and her normal attack becomes a melee strike.
Partner Skill Teepo Drain
2% chance with each hit to drain 10% of max HP and 5% TP. 5% more likely if the partner's HP is under 50%. 5% higher on bosses. 15% higher if the target is Technical. 20% more likely if the partner is under 50% TP. 30% more likely if the target is launched into the air.
